Things we build

We specialize in building fixed-wing aircraft, including gliders and trainers, as well as rotary-wing model like quadcopters for various applications.

RC planes

An RC (Radio-Controlled) plane is a model aircraft controlled remotely via a transmitter, used for recreational flying and aeromodelling.

Quadcopter

A Quadcopter is a type of drone that is powered by four motors, typically arranged in a square or X-shape, and is controlled remotely for tasks like aerial photograph or surveying.

Ornithopter

An Ornithopter is a type of aircraft that flies by mimicking the flapping motion of bird wings, using mechanical or electric systems to generate lift and thrust through wing movement.

Gliders

A glider is a lightweight, unpowered aircraft that relies on air currents and thermals to stay aloft. It is typically used for recreational flying, training, and aeronautical engineering experiments.

Hexacopter

A hexacopter is a drone powered by six motors, used for aerial photography, surveying, or as an experimental platform for various engineering projects.

FT Flyer

The FT Flyer is a beginner-friendly foamboard airplane designed by Flite Test. It's known for its ease of assembly and stable flight, great for learning aeromodelling basics.
